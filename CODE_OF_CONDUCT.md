# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We welcome participants of all backgrounds and experience levels.
We are committed to a professional, respectful, and collaborative environment.

## Expected behavior

Contributors should:

- communicate clearly and respectfully
- focus criticism on work, not people
- make decisions through documented rules and clear rationale
- ask for clarification when scope is ambiguous
- respect privacy and avoid leaking secrets or credentials

## Unacceptable behavior

Do not:

- harass or demean others
- use abusive language
- intentionally mislead on contracts, boundaries, or security assumptions
- perform unauthorized disclosure of secrets or secrets-like artifacts
- make repeated off-topic or disruptive interruptions

## Enforcement

Reports are acknowledged and reviewed quickly.
If a violation is severe, the issue is escalated by repository governance.

Maintainers may remove access or requests changes to contribution permissions.

## Scope

This conduct applies in all repo spaces, including discussions, PR comments, and review threads.
